(American) Football is back! Last week I made some adjustments to the dfs-helper repo [0] to make it compatible with the new urls and tables that Fantasy Pros are using this year for their projected score data. After scraping the data and running it through the solver, here was the suggested lineup for week 1 (generated on Saturday night):

Overall this roster did well, and scored 138.36 points in Yahoo DFS, which was enough to go 5-1 in quickmatches and place 8,807/85,200 in the NFL $1 Million Baller contest. I also put entries into the NFL League of Champs and NFL Yahoo Cup, where after round 1 it currently ranks 15,600/243,446 and 10,201/143,887 respectively. Not a bad start to the DFS season if I do say so myself.

I'm still just a bit salty, because the generated roster on Friday night had CEH at RB instead of Najee Harris, and the 49ers over the Packers at DST.. and those two changes would have been enough to go 6-0 and place higher in my other contests. Oh well, that's fantasy.

Onward to week 2.
